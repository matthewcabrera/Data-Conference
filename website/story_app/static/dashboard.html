Methods and Caveats

Entity Scope

Country-only analyses use metadata region classification to exclude aggregate entities. Region and income filters are then applied interactively.

Quality Percentiles

Within each year and indicator, countries are ranked and converted to percentiles where higher means better performance according to indicator directionality.

Wealth-Adjusted Residuals

Residuals come from indicator ~ log10(GNI per capita) fits by year. Positive z means better-than-expected performance relative to wealth.

Trend Movers

Top movers are sorted by quality-adjusted percent change from each country's first to last available year for the selected indicator.

Data: World Bank MDG Databank (2006-2015). Country-only filtering uses metadata region classification.
