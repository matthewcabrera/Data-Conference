# Story Voice Guardrails V5 (Expanded, Non-Padded)

Applies to `docs/story_script_v5.md`.

## Core Intent

Deliver EU-length depth without turning the script into a report. The medium is still scrollytelling: visuals carry orientation and magnitude; prose carries interpretation and stakes.

## Structural Contract

Use this six-beat architecture with a consolidated close:

1. `curve_mechanism` (teach line-distance grammar deeply)
2. `rwanda_trajectory` (pause beats + history texture + multi-indicator movement)
3. `vietnam_brief` (pattern confirmation, not a full section)
4. `us_comparison_arc` (decade persistence + multi-signal peer comparison)
5. `mirror_case` (Equatorial Guinea + Sierra Leone in one beat)
6. `what_changes_gap + close` (distribution dynamics + open questions + direct landing)

## Word Budgets

- Total script: `1700-2000`
- Pre-method body: `1550-1800`
- Methods block: `90-130`
- Beat ranges:
  - Beat 1: `280-340`
  - Beat 2: `260-330`
  - Beat 3: `110-160`
  - Beat 4: `320-390`
  - Beat 5: `120-180`
  - Beat 6 (includes close): `320-420`

## Narrative Mix Budget

Target (pre-method body):

- Teaching/narrative: `45%`
- Policy/institutions: `25%`
- Numbers in prose: `15%`
- Timeline/history: `10%`
- Methods language in body: `<=5%`
- Tolerance: `+/-5` points per bucket

## Non-Negotiable Cuts (Do Not Reintroduce)

1. `This is the mechanism for the rest of the piece.`
2. Rwanda residual decimals in body prose.
3. `The U.S. is the test case most readers will not expect.`
4. Parenthetical peer-count clutter in body prose.
5. `Now the mirror case.`
6. Long mirror synthesis sentence (keep terse; `Same failure.` style).
7. `If the line were destiny, this pattern would not exist.`
8. `Start with what the decade does and does not show.`
9. Three-line recap bridge before ending (`Growth raises potential...` form).

## Evidence Discipline

- Every quantitative statement in script body must map to `docs/story_script_traceability_v5.md`.
- No invented examples, no fictional people, no anecdotal composites.
- Causal caution appears in methods/endnote framing, not as repetitive hedge paragraphs in body.
- External context is allowed only as light framing and must map to `docs/story_context_endnotes_v5.md`.

## Visual-Text Interaction Rules

- Every major beat begins with `[VISUAL: ...]`.
- Visuals perform transitions; prose should not narrate obvious camera moves.
- Keep one persistent reference object: the income-life expectancy line.
- Numeric overload control: avoid >2 numeric clauses in a single sentence outside designated metric lines.

## U.S. Beat Minimum

Must include all of:

1. 2010 below-line snapshot.
2. 2006-2015 persistence (gap fails to close).
3. Maternal + infant + under-5 comparison versus high-income peers.
4. One named comparator country.

## Ending Rule

- No country recap list before close.
- Keep one closing sequence after the global-slider compression beat (no extra detour visual blocks).
- Land on constrained hope:
  - `The hopeful reading is not that progress is automatic. It is that progress is governable.`
  - `What happens next is a governance question, not a math question.`
