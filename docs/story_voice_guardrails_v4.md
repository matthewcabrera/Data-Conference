# Story Voice Guardrails V4 (EU-Length, No Padding)

Applies to `docs/story_script_v4.md`.

## Structural Rules

1. Keep the seven-beat order:
   - curve mechanism
   - Rwanda trajectory
   - Vietnam brief confirmation
   - U.S. comparison arc
   - Equatorial Guinea + Sierra Leone mirror
   - what-changes-the-gap
   - close
2. Keep one persistent visual grammar:
   - distance from income-life expectancy line.
3. Expansion is allowed only when it does one of:
   - teaches mechanism scale,
   - adds concrete repeated evidence,
   - opens a forward-looking question.

## Enforced Cuts (Do Not Reintroduce)

1. No "This is the mechanism for the rest of the piece."
2. No Rwanda residual decimals in prose.
3. No "The U.S. is the test case most readers will not expect."
4. No parenthetical peer-count clutter in body prose.
5. No "Now the mirror case."
6. Keep mirror synthesis to two words: `Same failure.`
7. No "If the line were destiny, this pattern would not exist."

## U.S. Beat Minimum Evidence

Must include all:

1. 2010 below-line snapshot.
2. 2006 -> 2015 persistence (gap does not close).
3. At least two additional indicator trends vs high-income peers.

## Style Rules

- Visual does navigation; prose does meaning.
- No z-scores in body text.
- No standalone caveat paragraph in body.
- Prefer short declarative lines after key number reveals.
- End without recap bullets.

## Title/Dek Rule

At least one title/dek option must foreground governability.
