# Story Voice Guardrails V3 (Single Mechanism)

Applies to `docs/story_script_v3.md`.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Keep one mechanism throughout:
   - `distance from income-life expectancy line`.
2. Keep one persistent reference visual:
   - the same scatter/line grammar, reused beat to beat.
3. Keep load-bearing countries to four:
   - Rwanda, United States, Equatorial Guinea, Sierra Leone.
4. Do not add full standalone sections for additive examples unless replacing an existing beat.
5. Remove connective filler transitions:
   - visuals execute the cut; prose adds meaning after the cut.
6. Delay interpretation after key numeric reveals:
   - at least one explicit pause beat in the Rwanda opening.
7. No recap list before closing line.

## U.S. Beat Requirements

Must include all three:

1. Position relative to line in 2010.
2. Persistence across time (2006 -> 2015 gap behavior).
3. One comparator trend in maternal mortality (U.S. vs at least one peer).

## Language Constraints

- No z-scores in narrative body.
- No methods/caveat paragraphs in body.
- One-sentence maximum for any causal-sounding interpretation.
- Prefer concrete verbs over abstractions (`drops`, `sits below`, `does not close`).

## Title/Dek Rule

At least one option must surface governability directly (`Progress Is Governable` framing).
